Health Tips to Prevent Stroke

Simple lifestyle actions can lower risk and support long-term wellbeing. Follow these evidence-based habits for better vascular health.

  • Engage in regular physical exercise, such as brisk walking, cycling, or swimming, for at least 30 minutes a day.
  • Practice relaxation techniques like yoga, meditation, or deep breathing to reduce stress and improve mental health.
  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats.
  • Limit salt intake and avoid high-sodium processed foods to support healthy blood pressure.
  • Monitor and control blood sugar and cholesterol levels through regular checkups.
  • Avoid smoking, limit alcohol intake, and keep a healthy body weight.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain consistent sleep patterns to support cardiovascular health.
